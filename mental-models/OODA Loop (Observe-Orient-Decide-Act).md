## Model ID: OODA_LOOP

## Logic Gate
* **Crisis Management**: Responding to a sudden production outage or a competitor's surprise launch.
* **Agile Processes**: Structuring how a team works in a highly uncertain, fast-moving environment.
* **Strategic Planning**: Navigating ambiguity where the "correct" answer isn't known upfront.
* Use this model to outmaneuver a problem or opponent by iterating through the learning cycle faster than the environment changes.

## Thinking Steps
1. **Observe**: Gather raw data from the environment immediately. What are the logs saying? What is the competitor doing? What is the user feedback? Do not judge, just collect.
2. **Orient**: Filter the raw data through your current mental models, cultural context, and past experiences. Synthesize the data into a situational awareness. What does this mean *right now*?
3. **Decide**: Formulate a hypothesis and select a specific course of action based on your orientation. What is the most likely intervention to succeed?
4. **Act**: Execute the decision swiftly and decisively. Do not wait for perfect information.
5. **Iterate**: The action will change the environment. Immediately return to Step 1 (Observe) to measure the impact and cycle through again. The goal is a faster tempo than the problem.

## State Output

Label the current phase as "OODA Phase: [Observe/Orient/Decide/Act]."

## Termination Criteria

Conclude a cycle with a concrete "Action Execution," and immediately prompt for new observations to begin the next loop.
